Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

Paneer, Butter, Cream, Tomato, Onion, Cashew, Spices

Process:

  1. Onion-tomato paste banao.
  2. Masale bhuno aur gravy ready karo.
  3. Paneer cubes daal ke 5 min simmer karo.
  4. Butter + cream daal ke serve karo.

Veg Biryani

Ingredients:

Basmati Rice, Mixed Veggies, Onion, Yogurt, Spices

Process:

  1. Rice 70% tak boil karo.
  2. Veg gravy banao yogurt + spices ke saath.
  3. Rice aur gravy ko layers me rakho.
  4. Dum pe 20 min pakao.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

Dosa Batter, Potato, Onion, Curry Leaves, Spices

Process:

  1. Batter se dosa bana ke spread karo.
  2. Potato masala ready karo onion + spices ke saath.
  3. Dosa ke beech masala fill karo.
  4. Coconut chutney aur sambar ke saath serve karo.

Chocolate Brownie

Ingredients:

Maida, Cocoa, Butter, Sugar, Eggs, Baking Powder

Process:

  1. Butter + sugar + eggs mix karo.
  2. Cocoa + maida + baking powder add karo.
  3. Batter ko mold me daal ke bake karo.
  4. Warm chocolate sauce ke saath serve karo.

Mango Smoothie

Ingredients:

Mango, Milk/Yogurt, Honey, Ice Cubes

Process:

  1. Mango cubes + yogurt + honey blender me daalo.
  2. Smooth blend karna hai.
  3. Ice cubes ke saath serve karo.
  4. Topping me mint leaves ya dry fruits daalo.
